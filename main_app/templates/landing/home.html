Our Commitment to Quality Education

At ElimCircuit, we strive to ensure that every child receives the education they deserve in every classroom across the circuit.

Our Vision

Quality Education for every child in every classroom in every school across the Elim Circuit. We believe in nurturing lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

  • Excellence in Teaching
  • Inclusive Education
  • Community Engagement
  • Innovative Learning
  • Continuous Improvement
  • Leadership Development

Schools of Specialisation (SOS) Assessment Dates

Schools of Specialisation cater to learners with above-average aptitude in specific fields. To be considered for placement, applicants are required to participate in a Placement Test and obtain an above-average mark. Fields of specialisation include Sports, Maths Science and ICT, Commerce and Entrepreneurship, Engineering, or Performing and Creative Arts. Click here for more information.

Elim Circuit Comprises 24 Schools Dedicated to Excellence

The Elim Circuit is home to 24 schools, each committed to providing quality education and fostering a strong learning environment for all students.

Services Offered by Elim Circuit

The Elim Circuit provides a variety of services to support the educational needs of its 24 schools, ensuring that every student has access to quality education and resources.

Curriculum Support

Providing guidance and resources to ensure that schools deliver the curriculum effectively, helping students achieve their full academic potential.

Teacher Training

Offering professional development and training programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of educators within the circuit.

Student Support

Providing counseling, mentorship, and additional resources to support students' academic and personal growth.

Resource Allocation

Ensuring that all schools within the circuit have access to the necessary educational materials and resources.

Infrastructure Support

Coordinating maintenance and development of school facilities to provide a safe and conducive learning environment.

Community Engagement

Fostering collaboration between schools and the community to enhance educational outcomes and support local development.
